Correlation Between Homeowners and Family Households

This graph attempts to illustrate a correlation between the number of homeowners that are family households (versus single households, renting or leasing). There is roughly 50-75% of homeowners that are owned by families 60-70% of the time. Of White Americans, a wide range of 40-100% are homeowners and of this range, 60-70% of homeowners are owned by families. Of Black Americans, 0-20% are homeowners and 60-70% of these homeowners are families. OF Asian Americans, 0-10% are homeowners with 60-70% being family owned.
